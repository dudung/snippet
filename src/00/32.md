# 0032
Solve 3 uknowns with 3 equations for $N_2(x, y)$ from $N_1(x, y)$?

$$\tag{1}
b_2 = \left[ \frac{ (y_3 - y_1) }{ (x_2 - x_3)(y_3 - y_1) - (x_3 - x_1)(y_2 - y_3) } \right].
$$

$$\tag{2}
c_2 = - b_2 \left( \frac{x_3 - x_1}{y_3 - y_1} \right).
$$

$$\tag{3}
a_2 = 1 - b_2 x_2 + c_2 y_2.
$$
