# 0015
Asymptotic values of sigmoid function, the form with $x_0$.

$$\tag{1}
\sigma(x) = \frac{1}{1 + e^{-k(x - x_0)}}.
$$

Values of Eqn (1) are

$$\tag{2}
\sigma(x) = \left\{
\begin{array}{ll}
0, & x << x_0, \newline
\tfrac12, & x = x_0, \newline
1, & x >> x_0.
\end{array}
\right.
$$
